The CM_LIBRARY functions are designed to be portable among not only UNIX
systems, but also (largely) to VAX/VMS.  On that system, the common-library
tree is installed as CM_LIBRARY (there it requires the PORTUNIX tree):
	VAX/VMS 5.3-1 -- CC 3.2
